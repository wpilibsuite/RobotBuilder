Motor Controller

What is it?

A motor controller that uses PWM signals to control the speed of the attached motor. There are twelve main PWM motor controllers available for use in FRC: Victor, Talon, Jaguar, Victor SP, Talon SRX, Spark, SD540, Victor SPX, DMC 60, SPARK Max, Talon FX, Venom Smart Motor, and SPARK Flex.

Properties

Type
The type of motor controller to use.
Output Port (PWM)
The PWM port on the roboRIO or MXP that the output is plugged into. Ports 0-9 are on the roboRIO and 10-19 are on the MXP.
Follow
The Motor Controller to follow the commands of
Inverted
Whether to invert the direction of the motor controller.
