Mecanum Drive

What is it?

Mecanum drive is a method of driving using specially designed wheels with rollers at 45 degree angles that allow the robot to drive in any direction without changing the orientation of the robot. A robot with a conventional drivetrain (four or six wheels) must turn in the direction it needs to drive. A mecanum robot can move in any direction without first turning and is also called a holonomic drive.

Properties

Left Front Motor
The motor to use as the front left motor for robot drive actions.
Left Rear Motor
The motor to use as the rear left motor for robot drive actions.
Right Front Motor
The motor to use as the front right motor for robot drive actions.
Right Rear Motor
The motor to use as the rear right motor for robot drive actions.
Maximum Output
Maximum allowable output to the motors, generally this should be one.
Safety Enabled
Whether or not motor safety should be in effect
Safety Expiration Time
How long before motor safety kicks in.
